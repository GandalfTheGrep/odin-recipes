Lammelår

A leg of organic Icelandic Lamb cooking over a fire pit

Ingredients

  • 1 leg of lamb
  • Honey
  • Mustard
  • Cloves of garlic, sliced, inserted into pockets slit by a sharp knife (optional).
  • Sprigs of freshly picked rosemary
  • Salt and pepper (optional)
  • Use non poisonous leaves
  1. First, insert sliced garlic into pockets.
  2. Cover the leg of lamb with honey and mustard, salt, freshly gorund pepper, and freshley snipped parsley
  3. Wrap the entire lamb well in leaves
  4. Get Willing helpers to dig the cooking pit about a half a meter (20 inches) deep
  5. Fill hole with rocks and light fire on top, heat rocks till glowing red
  6. Place meat in the middle of the pit, and cook for 1hr 15mins
  7. Serve with mead and drunken singing
