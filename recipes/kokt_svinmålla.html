Kokt Svinmålla

Boiled lambsquarters

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fresh, very young, tender lambsquarters
  • 2/3 Cup Water
  • Dash or two of salt
  1. Rinse the Lambsquarters
  2. Add the salt to the water, adn bring to a boil.
  3. Add in the Lambsquarters and boil for 5 minutes
  4. Pour off the liquid and allow the lambsquarters to drain
  5. Serve with a little butter.
